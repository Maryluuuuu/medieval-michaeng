## Chapter 7 ✅ Written (full Spanish draft)

**Title:** "The Valley Between Hills" **Narrative time:** Two days **Theme:** Walls coming down. Breathing chapter — the quietest in the fic. Visual thread: hands. **Key events:**

- **Scene 1 — 2yeon "The Report":** First real scene for the couple. Jeongyeon reports on the stables. Cricket restless before the fire. Half a centimeter on the desk. "Take care of yourself. The kingdom needs you whole." Jeongyeon draws the line.
- **Scene 2 — The move:** Back to the royal wing. Stolen books relocated. Door left ajar.
- **Scene 3 — The request:** Permission chain (6 sub-scenes). Mina asks to leave the castle for the first time. "I've never left. Not once in twenty-one years." Nayeon approves with conditions. "My mother is going to love you" — Chaeyoung thinks _my princess_ and doesn't correct herself. Tzuyu: "This is exactly a formal introduction."
- **Scene 4 — Outside the walls:** Mina's first time outside the castle. East gate. Brisa (the mare). "It's bigger than I imagined." Subtle manifestation: grass sways without wind.
- **Scenes 5–7 — Bryndell:** Visit to Miyoung. Sign language — Mina speaks fluently. Miyoung's maternal assessment. She recognizes in Mina the same expression Hajoon had. Request: "take care of her." Miyoung laughs — the longest laugh in months. "Her greatest fear isn't dying. It's that you die first."
- **Scene 8 — Goodbye:** Miyoung hugs Mina. Hill on the way back: "Not today. Today my name is Mina." Chaeyoung says "Mina" plainly.
- **Scene 9 — First lesson:** Terrace, candle, tea. Mina teaches Chaeyoung sign language. Mina takes Chaeyoung's hands to correct finger position — three seconds of contact. "When you say it to your mother, make sure it's true." Door ajar: the space between _almost_ and _all the way_ is a little smaller than before.
