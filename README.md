# Medieval MiChaeng

A medieval fantasy fanfic based on TWICE. Main pairing: MiChaeng (Mina x Chaeyoung).

This repository serves as the organizational hub for the project — character profiles, timeline, plot threads, worldbuilding, and canon tracking are all maintained here to keep the story consistent as it grows.

## Synopsis

In a kingdom where an ancient prophecy foretells that the third daughter of the twentieth generation of the royal family will possess immense power, Princess Mina — known as "The Silent Princess" — has spent her entire life within the castle walls. When the threats against her become real, knight Son Chaeyoung is assigned as her personal guard.

What begins as duty becomes complicity, and what begins as complicity becomes something neither of them knows how to name.
