## MAIN NARRATIVE THREADS

### Investigation / Internal threat

- **Sir Callen:** Neutralized in Ch. 6 (wound to the side, captured). Verbalized the anti-prophecy motivation: "What we do is necessary. The prophesied one is a threat to the entire kingdom." First time an antagonist speaks.
- **Infiltration network:** Knife with a lot mark (military commission, 8 months ago, 20 units, paid in cash, no buyer name). The blacksmith only works with officers of knight rank or higher. Who paid?
- **The four allied traitors:** Arrived with the autumn rotation. Two in the east wing, two in the south wing. Operate in pairs. Neutralized in Ch. 6.
- **The mailbox stone:** Months of use. Since before the attack, possibly before Chaeyoung arrived. Who else in the castle is connected?
- **Ch. 3 prisoner:** Professional silence during interrogation. Still alive? Did they talk later?

### Mina's powers

- **Warmth in the palms:** Months before Ch. 5. No apparent pattern. Confirmed in the books: "Warmth in the palms."
- **Early manifestations:** Cups that move, pages that turn on their own, breeze in closed rooms.
- **Awakening (Ch. 6):** Increased gravity, women lifted off the ground, cracking ribs, flameless candle. Uncontrolled.
- **Post-awakening (Ch. 7):** Latent mode. Subtle manifestation outside the castle (grass sways without wind). Power responds to openness/space.
- **Nature:** Pure energy — creation and destruction. Not elemental. This is what makes the prophecy so threatening.
- **Stolen books:** _On the Nature of the Unborn Power_, blue-edged volume, _Documented Cases of Late Manifestation_. Stored in a drawer under clothing (royal wing). The handwritten notebook mentions additional unretrieved texts.

### Prophecy

- The third daughter of three sisters, twentieth generation of the royal family, will possess immense power.
- Nayeon explains it in Ch. 2. She doesn't believe in it, but knows others do — some as a blessing, others as a threat.
- Callen and his faction see it as an existential threat to the kingdom.

### Night Veil / Chaeyoung's sword

- Ancient order sworn to the prophecy, not to kings. Faded over the centuries.
- Sword forged with Nocturnio (metal from the Grey Mountains). Engravings = protection spells. Reacts to threats against the third daughter (glows, vibrates).
- Chaeyoung inherited the blood: superhuman reflexes, extreme endurance, sense of danger. Intensified near Mina. She attributes it to experience/adrenaline.
- Vakren: enhanced vision in extreme danger (Ch. 6 — sees Callen's pulse, wrist tremor). Pre-Verenthaal, not yet named.
- Hajoon (father) knew something, planned to investigate. Died before telling her.

### Letter to the great-grandmother

- Sent in Ch. 6. In transit.
- The great-grandmother arrives in Ch. 9. Brings Verenthaal Phase 2 (Adelais, Neyrene, Vyrnhert, Blothren, Razliv, Tikhaya).
